About Us

Who we are?

Children Heaven is a non-profit infant center and pre-school specializing in learning through authentic experiences and nurturing respectful relationships.

What do we do?

We focuses on cultivating a positive attitude towards learning and on instilling good values in our students.

Where we are ?

As on date, we have started our initiative from the heart of our country i.e Delhi(Nangloi).

What's next ?

Our goal is to provide a strong educational foundation, allowing our students to cultivate a love of learning, willingness to help others, and creativity and discipline in all their endeavors.
